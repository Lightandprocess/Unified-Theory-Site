Introduction

This theory proposes a unique model connecting black holes, white holes, dark matter, the nature of light, and conscious experience as fundamental components in the cycle of universal creation and observation.

Core Concepts

  • Black holes consume matter and convert it to pure energy.
  • White holes may be the starting points of new universes—Big Bangs.
  • Light is the singularity—able to be compressed infinitely and tied to the birth of new reality.
  • Dark Matter is theorized to be dead light—informationless energy no longer in cycle.
  • Human consciousness gives time its dimension through observation and emotional relativity.

This theory is a synthesis of observational astrophysics, thermodynamics, consciousness philosophy, and speculative cosmology.
